El Río Verde

Río Verde is located in a rural area on the outskirts of Baños and the jungle, and to reach the site you don't necessarily need a tour guide, as the place is very well signposted. At the end of the river, you will find the Pailón del Diablo waterfall, a beautiful place to visit.

Rio Blanco

Río Blanco originates on the western slopes of the Pichincha volcano and flows down the mountains of Mindo. To enter the province of Esmeraldas, it receives waters from the Río Toachi. On its right side, its tributaries flow into the Río Caoní, and on the left side, into the Río Quinindé. Its tributaries are, on the right bank, the Río Caoní, and on the left bank, the Río Quinindé.
